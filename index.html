Breaking Bad

A journey into crime, morality, and transformation.

Series Summary

Breaking Bad follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine producer, as he partners with former student Jesse Pinkman. The series explores morality, family, and the consequences of choices.

Main Characters

Walter White

A chemistry teacher who transforms into the drug kingpin 'Heisenberg'.

Jesse Pinkman

Walter’s former student and partner in the meth business.

  • Walter’s cancer diagnosis

Events by Season

Season 1

The first season focuses on Walter White’s diagnosis and his entry into the meth business with Jesse Pinkman.

  • Walter’s cancer diagnosis
  • First meth cook with Jesse
  • Conflict with local drug dealers
  • Birth of the “Heisenberg” persona
  • Family tensions begin

Season 2

The second season expands the conflict, introduces Saul Goodman, and ends with a tragic event.

  • Expansion of Walter and Jesse’s drug trade
  • Introduction of Saul Goodman
  • Strained relationship between Walter and Skyler
  • Jesse’s drug addiction
  • The tragic plane crash

Season 3

Season three introduces Gus Fring and escalates the danger with the Mexican cartel.

  • Skyler discovers Walter’s secret
  • Gus Fring offers Walter a deal
  • Jesse’s emotional struggles
  • Threat from the Mexican cartel
  • Bloody showdown with the cartel

Season 4

The fourth season builds to the ultimate showdown between Walter and Gus Fring.

  • Walter and Jesse working in Gus’s lab
  • Gus tries to win Jesse’s loyalty
  • Walter’s plan to eliminate Gus
  • The explosive final confrontation
  • Walter’s full transformation into Heisenberg

Season 5

The final season shows Walter’s empire, Hank’s discovery, and Walter’s downfall.

  • Walter builds his drug empire
  • Jesse distances himself from Walter
  • Hank discovers Walter is Heisenberg
  • Conflict with new gangs and cartels
  • The tragic ending and Walter’s fall
